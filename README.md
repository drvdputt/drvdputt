### Who am I?

Hi, I am Dries Van De Putte, currently an astronomer at Western University, Canada. This is my Github profile, where I share some of the code that I use for scientific analysis. More info at the main website [drvdputt.github.io](https://drvdputt.github.io).

### Projects and codes

Member of the data reduction team for [PDRs4All](https://pdrs4all.org/), utilities for running the JWST MIRI MRS and NIRSpec IFU pipelines in the [PDRs4All package](https://github.com/drvdputt/jwst-pdr-reduction)

Actively working on [PAHFIT](https://github.com/PAHFIT/pahfit), and developing [PAHFIT-cube](https://github.com/drvdputt/PAHFIT-cube).

During past projects, I contributed to and used [SKIRT](https://github.com/SKIRT/SKIRT9) and the [BEAST](https://github.com/BEAST-Fitting/beast).

Main developer of [RADAGAST](https://github.com/drvdputt/RADAGAST), written and coupled to SKIRT during my PhD.
